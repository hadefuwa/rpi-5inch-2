Clear Service Worker Cache

Use this page to clear all cached files and force a fresh reload.

What this does:

  • Clear All Caches: Removes all cached files from the browser
  • Unregister Service Worker: Removes the service worker completely
  • Reload Page: Refreshes the page to see changes

After clearing cache:

  1. Click "Clear All Caches"
  2. Click "Unregister Service Worker"
  3. Go back to your main page
  4. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
